OYiM Excutive Sumarry

One Year in Mission (OYiM) JLC-WIUM began our broadcasting works back in March and the rest is a history to remember in this unprecedented year. Our committed team is driven by the opportunities to meet the urgent needs of the urban community in Jakarta and beyond through virtual connection.

There was never a dull moment but all worked well according to God's plan. From the selection and appointment of the strategic team to the strategic expansion of OYiM movement to meet its end goals, church planting project and center of influence initiative. Our calling is to use Christ's method alone to fulfill God given commissions through "Tell the World" to "I Will Go" worldwide church strategic plans to proclaim the three angels' messages in our generation.
